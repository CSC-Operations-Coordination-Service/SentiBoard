Terms and Conditions

The objectives of the Sentinel Online technical website are to allow users to find all the information they need regarding the Copernicus Sentinel programme, the individual Sentinel missions, their operational focus and the thematic areas they support. Particular care is given to explanations of the instruments, the performance of data products including scientific applications, Ground Segment processing, and guidance on accessing Sentinel data.

In addition, the website presents news and technical documentation related to the Sentinel missions.
